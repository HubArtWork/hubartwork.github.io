About lip pigments

The Liquid Powder Lip Set includes 4 bright lipstick colors and 4 pastel natural colors, 3 of which are natural lip colors designed to rebuild faded lip color.

There are 3 shades of natural lips colors: neutral, warm and cold.

Nude Beige can be used as a standalone color to create pale lips, or as a base to create light, natural lip colors. Just add 1 drop of bright colors to 10 drops of Nude Beige, and you will get 4 beautiful, light natural lip colors.

Bright colors can be used either as standalone lipstick colors or as brightening additions to natural colors.

Pumpkin can also be used as a dark lip corrector.

Pigment ingredients are distilled water, alcohol, glycerin and a concentrated amount of coloring particles. All ingredients are listed on an FDA-approved list of colorants.
