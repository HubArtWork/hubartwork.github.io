Garaunteed to fade natural brown

If you keep seeing unnatural, faded eyebrow colors like grey, blue, pink or orange on your clients’ eyebrows when they come back in for touch-ups, then it’s time for you to switch to Liquid Powder pigments.

For Liquid Powder eyebrow formulations, we used only inorganic (mineral) pigments that are produced by a unique method of calcination (roasting at high temperatures) which results in beautiful browns with yellow undertones.

All of our eyebrow formulations are made of 100% brown minerals of color that you can see right in the bottle. Only a couple of lighter colors have a small amount of yellow or white added.

There is no red, black, blue or green in eyebrow formulas – only concentrated brown. All of our browns will, if matched correctly to the right skin tone, fade through a lighter version of the same color.

Properties of liquid powder pigments

Liquid Powder pigments have high light fastness, meaning they don’t fade easily in the sun and have high bleed resistance. This also means that your eyebrow hairstrokes or eyeliner tail will not become blurry and smudgy over time.

Liquid Powder is very concentrated and made up of simple ingredients like distilled water, alcohol, glycerin and concentrated iron oxide. All of the ingredients are listed on an FDA-approved list of colorants.

Liquid powder pigments are vegan, cruelty-free, and not tested on animals.
