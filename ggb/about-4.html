The Liquid Powder Mineral (Inorganic) Color Correctors Set is designed to neutralize undesirable colors and achieve natural-looking shades of eyebrows. They can also be added in small amounts to other pigments to intensify desirable tones.

Orange
Orange

An orange color modifying pigment.

Used for color corrections of grey or blue eyebrow colors.

Additionally, 2-3 drops added to any brown pigment will prevent greying in very cool skin tones.

Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol, Distilled Water, Glycerin, CI 77491 (Orange)
Color Uses Turns saturated grey into brown.
Yellow-Green
Yellow-Green

A light greenish-taupe color modifying pigment.

Used for color corrections of orange-red eyebrow colors.

It contains enough yellow to not pull too much grey.

Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol, Distilled Water, Glycerin, CI 77289 (Green), CI 77492 (Yellow)
Color Uses Turns orange-red into brown.
Yellow
Yellow

A mustard color modifying pigment.

Used for color corrections of violet or grey, unsaturated eyebrow colors.

Can be used for color lightening.

Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol, Distilled Water, Glycerin, CI77492 (Yellow)
Color Uses Turns violet back to brown. Used for lightening.
Red-Brown
Red-Brown

A red-brown modifying pigment.

Used for color corrections of unsaturated grey, greenish-grey or bluish grey eyebrows color. If the unwanted color is very saturated use Orange.

Can be used as a standalone product to create very warm, reddish-brown eyebrows.

Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol, Distilled Water, Glycerin, CI 77491 (Red), CI 77499 (Brown)
Color Uses Turns unsaturated grey into brown.
