About our PIXEL needles

The most important characteristic for cartridges is safety.
PIXEL eliminates the possibility of cross-contamination and ensures you do not jeopardize your client’s health.

Full Membrane

Our cartridges have a full membrane that helps prevent the pigment from backflowing.
That means the pigment will not be able to flow back through the other end of the cartridge into your machine, contaminating your device with body fluids and putting your clients’ health at risk.

Quality Needles

In order to perform a quality procedure, you must have a good quality needle made of medical-grade stainless steel with no bent needle tips that will damage your client’s skin.

PIXEL needles are made of 316-grade stainless steel and medical-grade plastic.
The quality of each needle is maintained by multiple, thorough checkpoints, including a 1000HD electron microscope.
