How do I update credit card details?
  • Login into your Proofactive account -> click “Billing” in the sidebar -> Click “Usage”
  • On the page that opens, click “Add proofs.”
  • Select from the list of available packages to buy -> confirm your selection in opened window.
  • We will charge payment method on file accordingly.
  • Extra proofs and proofs you bought outside of your plan never expire. You can find the number of available extra proofs on your “Usage” page.
  • Extra proofs are non-refundable, for more details please see our Terms of Use
