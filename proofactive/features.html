Proofactive Features

Share files, big or small

  • Our mobile-optimized proofs are ready to be securely shared among your team or to your clients. Your clients don’t even need to have a Proofactive account.
  • One of the benefits of Proofactive is that proof files you share always stay up-to-date, letting you, your team and clients remain on the same page.
  • You can use email, messenger services, or your own website to utilize our API.

Easy and intuitive proof builder tool

  • Proofs built for your business's unique needs.
  • Customize our templates, or use our intuitive form builder to set up proofs that work for your industry from shippers to dealership to real estate - and keep your customers updated on every device so everyone remains on the same page.
  • With templates that take less than 5 minutes to create, we offer a stress-free option that doesn’t require any coding.

Drawing tools

  • Take a picture and mark flaws, damages and anything you want to point out. Use various tools, such as pencils, circles, and arrows with different color options.
  • Add short text comments over a picture.
  • Immediately see a preview of your marks and comments befores submitting.

Reliable storage and quick access

  • Your files are optimized for faster transfer, all without compromising image quality.
  • Proof files are conveniently saved on cloud servers to ensure security and reliability.
  • Quick secure access by you, your team and clients from anywhere in the world.

Simplify team management

  • Organize your proof files in a simple, easy to use and intuitive application.
  • Admin controls enable you to track all user activities as well as secure access to shared data.

Proof of delivery

  • All proof photos, comments, scans, etc. are automatically recorded with geolocation to ensure accuracy.
  • Signature pad ready for any type of proof. You can request a signed proof of acceptance using our mobile app or website.

Keep your data safe

  • Proofactive security features let you control exactly who has access to your files, delete proofs or users, and recover files if you need to.
  • We take uptime seriously, so we offer reliable access for your account and clients without interruptions.

Timeline

  • Use proof to track notifications for your customers through the shipping process or just for tracking remote locations.
  • Customers can track progress by checking their link or email.

API and documentation

  • Powerful APIs that enable you to send, receive and track proof changes effortlessly.
  • Our APIs are focused on simplicity and compliance to standards.
