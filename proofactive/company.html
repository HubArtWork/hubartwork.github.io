Our company

Proofactive is the world’s first predictive and intelligence proof exchange platform to track damages and flaws of goods while in transit. As the #1 proof of delivery and photo bill of lading provider, we’ve unified the latest technology and traditional milestones of the shipping and logistics business.

Smart and Predictive Proofs

We also offer proof tracking for non-shipping and delivery companies, such as business with remote locations that need monitoring.

For years, Proofactive has empowered companies to have trackable proofs ready at any time with real-time data, photos and recorded geolocation at their fingertips.
