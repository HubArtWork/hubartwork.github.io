Calls & Call forwarding

Mobile integration:

Possibility to call / receive calls by browser, cell phone, mobile application.

Call trace:

You have access to the history of all your calls.

Call transfer:

You can transfer the call to another line or extension.

Click to call:

You can make calls from the app or the site and you just have to click on the call made button on the platform.

Speed Dial:

You can set up a button that will automatically call the desired person.

Answer from anywhere

Remote Office:

You only need the internet to have access to your office with all your messages, calls, etc.

Directory:

You have access to the list of all employees and their numbers so that you can easily transfer the call.​

Mobile App:

You can manage your voicemail, messages, status, extensions and many more options from the app on your mobile device.

Displays & Conferences

Caller ID:

You can display the company number or any other number when you call.

Hold:

Possibility to pause music / pause message (promotions)

Call Park:

You can pause the call so that you can continue the call on another device.

Call waiting:

You can pause your call so that you can answer the other.

3 way calling:

You can add a third number to the call.

Call Blocking:

You can block any number.

Out of Service Call Forwarding:

Simultaneous Ring:

Ringing on multiple devices at the same time.
