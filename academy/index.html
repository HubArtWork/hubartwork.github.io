What sets GGB training apart

We teach advanced, non-painful, and safe methods of application. We have developed our own brand of top-quality needle cartridges, drawing tools, and equipment for PMU application. 3-day Ombré Powder Brows training

GGB Ombré Powder Brows

GGB Ombré Powder Brows are hyperrealistic, 3D eyebrow tattoos created by means of color gradient, light, and dark shadows without defined contours. As a result, eyebrows look full and symmetrical but very natural.

The GGB Powder Brows can last 1-3 years before gradually fading away through lighter brown shades.

Unlike microblading, this procedure is very gentle to the skin, does not create scarring, can be done on all skin types (even the most sensitive and sun-damaged skin), and can be reapplied every couple of years.

GGB Ombré Powder Brows procedure

GGB Powder Brows procedure is a unique 3D brow-shading technique that includes a detailed method of sketching and non-painful machine pigment application. The GGB Powder Brows are created with the aid of high-quality GGB brand tools and pigments. Powerful equipment delivers pigment without creating extra trauma to the skin, allowing for fast, easy healing with good pigment retention and little visual downtime.

Clean mineral Liquid Powder pigments have muted natural brown colorsthat fade gracefully over time. The GGB 3D Powder Brows procedure is theideal brow-enhancing application, one that is growing in popularity around the world — and taking the place of manual, more traumatizing procedures such as microblading.

GGB Ombré Powder Brows training

GGB Ombre Powder Brows training is not just a regular PMU training course. During our course, students go through multiple learning stages, starting with basic hygiene rules, setting up the working environment, and dress code and communication skills followed by detailed theoretical and hands-on training in permanent makeup.

Students meet in a classroom setting for practical training and learn drawing skills, practicing first on latex pads and then on live models so that students gain firsthand experience with the actual procedures under supervision of the GGB trainers. 3-day Lip Blush training

GGB Lip Blush

GGB Lip Blush refers to a method of lip re-colorizing that is designed to restore natural lip color, providing a fresh, healthy, and more-defined appearance without the heavy makeup look.

GGB Lip Blush procedure

The GGB Lip Blush procedure is a unique, highly detailed method of sketching and non-painful machine pigment application.

The GGB Lip Blush is created with the aid of high-quality GGB brand tools and pigments.

Powerful equipment delivers pigment without creating extra trauma to the skin, allowing for fast, easy healing with good pigment retention and little to no visual downtime. Our mixable pigments have a large selection of colors so that you can match or recreate any natural lip color.

GGB Lip Blush training
