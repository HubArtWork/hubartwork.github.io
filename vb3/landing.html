Features

Fast and accurate translation of the audio track for the video

Vidby uses artificial intelligence, which automatically translates videos with 90% accuracy. You can choose male or female voice for your video. Vidby guarantees 100% synchronization of video and voice.

Fast and accurate translation of the audio track for the video

Vidby offers an option to upload your personal transcription to the video or use our service to manually refine the original transcription.So you get 99-100% translation quality within 24 hours.

Free subtitles with translation accuracy of 99-100%

Vidby automatically creates free subtitles for each new video in the translated language.
You get subtitles as a bonus.
