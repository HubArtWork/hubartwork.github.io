Welcome
to Coral Energy

Coral Energy started in 2010 as a product trader. We have evolved into an international trading organisation with an extensive global presence covering oil and gas, petrochemicals, fertilisers and specialised dry bulk products.

Coral Energy is firmly committed to the clean energy of the future, as an investor and technology developer.
