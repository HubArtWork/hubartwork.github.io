About Practithink

PractiThink is an online educational platform for students, parents and teachers. We offer hundreds of online test preparation and skill building with interactive exercises, mini or full tests, printable learning material for children from pre-k to 5th grade. Many children learn Maths, English, Science, Social studies with us improving their knowledge and skills. Don’t know where to start yet? Check your child’s math skill level and select a skill improving plan with PractiThink.

OUR MISSION

At Practithink, we are passionate about creating and supporting the best educational technology possible.

Thanks to our unique and knowledgeable products, children and adults from all over the world learn and improve their Math skills. Our commitment to solving the challenges of children and teachers bring effective and goal oriented results. We love helping students from all over the world to improve their Math knowledge.

OUR philosophy

The major keys to a confident student and good test taker are their problem-solving skills. Practice makes perfect.

Honest and supportive approaches

We support your suggestions and involvement in our methods. At PractiThink, we always welcome commitment, engagement, and improvement as it helps us provide high-level and reliable content

Encouraged tutors

No matter the specialty, whether a school teacher or a parent, we created a platform that inspires your children and students to improve their knowledge in various subjects. We encourage all teachers and tutors to provide the best educational sources to kids by presenting the most motivational platform that can be used at home, at school, or anywhere you prefer.

Facilitated educational programs

Teaching is by far one of the most compound and demanding jobs. We have gathered an outstanding team to create digital resources, helpful tools, and training materials for your children to have the most convenient learning platform. Our approaches are easy to use in every teaching phase, and they meet the national education standards.

Individual practices

Each child is different and has different challenges and aims. Therefore, we deliver unique materials that can fit all kinds of requirements and educational needs. With this, kids are gaining more confidence in their practices and show higher results.
