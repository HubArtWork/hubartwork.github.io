Why should I rely on astrology if it is not even a science?

The fact is that nowadays most people have an incorrect understanding of astrology. It is not so fatal, and people always have a choice in their actions. Yes, the influence of stars and planets on humans has not been proven, but since ancient times some patterns have been deduced. Thus, events of a certain type happened exactly during the period of a specific location of celestial objects in the zodiac circle.

That’s quite easy. For example, if you have any doubts about the upcoming trip, choose seats on an airplane, train or other means of transportation, focusing on your lucky numbers. Why does Aurora ask about my date, time and place of birth?

We prioritize individual approaches to each user. Thus, at the first step, Aurora requests detailed data of your birth in order to build the most personalized forecast for you and give you the information as closer to the truth as possible.

Let Aurora know about this at the very beginning of using the app. Also, you can always ask your relatives about his information with or request the data from the hospital where you were born. Moreover, you have the opportunity to contact a professional astrologer, as today a lot of specialists can clarify the time of your birth.
