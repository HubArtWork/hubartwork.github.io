Aurora will show you that astrology is much more useful than you think

The app contains a set of tools that will help you solve your life issues. Let us explain what we mean.

In the pursuit of success in work and personal life, we often forget who we really are. Each of us is looking for tools for self-identification. Our attention is most focused on ourselves, our values, feelings, purpose and fulfillment. We have become more selfish, so we try to analyze ourselves and our actions more carefully.

We are not indifferent to people. Because of this our team began to study all possible options to help ourselves and others. Then the knowledge of western astrology opened up to us. We realized that we had the wrong idea about this subject. We found out that with an individual approach, Astropsychology can really help to discover one's true personality.

At this point the idea came to us to create a product that could promote Astrology to the masses. Nevertheless, that knowledge was not enough. So our team began to expand and we hired professionals from the field.

Later we became interested in lunar cycles, calendars and their influence on our planet. It turned out that lunar days have different energies too. And then we wondered what would happen if we combined this information with astrological principles? Here was the second shock: it turned out that these predictions really do work.
