1919

The fallen mask

In an old house, somewhere in a mystical world, a pizza chef was working on creating the perfect crust.

After many tries and different combinations for the crust, he gives up. He felt like nothing ed. He took all the crusts and threw them out the window to feed the birds.
1919

Survival of the fittest

In an old house, somewhere in a mystical world, a pizza chef was working on creating the perfect crust.

The birds ate all the pizza crusts. Only one survived. That same day, the pizza chef left his small town and said goodbye to everybody.
2019

Nothing ever leaves
the earth

100 years later, a little boy walks a path by the old house.

Where the pizza chef used to live, and found a crust lying on the ground, in perfect condition. He picks it up and brings it home.
2019

Finding good works

He places the crust in a jar and went on a mission to find out where it comes from.Since no one lived in that old house for almost 100 years.

He starts asking questions to a lot of people in the small town until one old man tells him that there was a very known pizza chef living in that house. That old man didn't understand how a crust can keep its freshness that long after 100 years.
2019

Where fous goes.
Energy flows.

They decided to visit the old house to try to understand where that crust comes from.

They go in and find hundreds of papers in garbage bins with crust's ingredients and realized the chef was working on a new crust ingredient. They found a few papers on the pizza chef's desk and decided to try and reproduce that magical perfect crust.
2019

Crusty Crust

100 years later, a little boy walks a path by the old house.

Finally, after 1 year, they succeeded. They found that paper that contained the ingredients for that perfect crust. It was named Crusty Crust.
2020

Start of a new journey

They opened a pizza place in their town and named it after that perfect crust's name, Crusty Crust.

Using only ingredients of that perfect crust to make pizzas, the town is enjoying pizza like never before. It's so delicious, they are even eating the crust.
