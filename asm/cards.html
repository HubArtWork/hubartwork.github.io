Local moving

Mega Moving Service handles all moves with the same effort and consideration whether is be to the next neighborhood or next state. It is our goal to make your move as easy as possible. We treat your belongings with the most professional care, for we use top quality packing materials, employ skilled moving associates, and transport with clean and well-equipped trucks. American Moving service wants to provide you with the most complete and worry-free move. We will supply you with all the necessary tools to move your possessions quickly and easily.

Mega Moving Service offers for Local Moving:

  • A free estimate before any of your items is moved to the new location. The estimate can be done in person, over the phone, or by email. A detailed list of items to be moved will be completed and a price will be configured.
  • Free box delivery to your front door.
  • Packing and crating of items, if necessary. Blanket wrapping of all furnishings and special consideration is made to valuables such as fine art, antiques, and other similar valuable items.
  • Timely pick-up of all items to be moved by our concerned staff. A foreman accompanies the staff of movers to ensure that all items are carefully packed and loaded. The foreman keeps track of the necessary paperwork.
  • Unloading of your possessions to the new location. All items will be placed where you desire and will be safely executed using special moving equipment.
  • Free basic insurance with all local moves.
  • No extra charges for moving on the weekend.

Local moves are charged by the hour. There are no additional charges for stairs and/or elevators.
