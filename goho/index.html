Problems We Solve

  • Business
  • Guests
  • Staff
Share your menu online

Signup, create a menu and share it with a QR-code or a link. Good opportunity for small businesses without a website.

Share your menu online

Decrease your printing costs with e-menu. Deploy your menu with us for free, don't pay developers for a website.

Increase productivity of waiters

The ordering process is automatized by the App. Waiters only deliver orders most of the time. They are unloaded and can be engaged somewhere else.

Speed up your service

Your guests don't need a waiter to make an order. They come, take a seat, scan the QR code, read your menu, make a choice and press the Order button.

Receive feedback

Your guests can leave a feedback using the App.

Get online payments

Your guests can pay with the App whenever they wish to. They can also add tips

Exclude human factor mistakes

Orders are controlled by AI. Your guests get what they ordered. Waiters don't take part in ordering process and simply have no chance for a mistake.

Suspend unavailable menu items

If a menu item is unavailable for some reason, a manager or a waiter can suspend it so that the guests don't see it in the menu.
