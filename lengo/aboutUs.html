Our Loan Process

Initial Contact

  • Borrower contacts LendVent LLC.
  • Loan assessment
  • Data gathering: experience level, financial, and credit background of the borrower, the current and future value of the project and will ask for a summary of the proposed deal.
  • Issuance of Proof of Funds

Underwriting

  • Fill out a loan application
  • Provide a purchase contract for the property
  • Provide a written anticipated scope of work for the property
When the above is completed:
  1. we will schedule an appraiser.
  2. Bank Statement
  3. Income and credit.
  4. project contractor or builder
  5. property title history
  6. property insurance.

Loan Closing

Closing attorney and/or Title co are assigned to close a loan.
Documents needed to be signed at closing/settlement:

  1. mortgage, note, security agreement, personal guarantee, investment affidavit and assignment of rents and leases.
  2. proceeds are wired to the settlement agent,
  3. documents are signed and reviewed by the various parties,
  4. disbursement of loan proceeds is approved.

Servicing (Draw Management)

  • On a rehabilitation or new construction loan, funds are released in stages of the loan dedicated to the renovation or construction project.
  • Each installment will be released to the borrower’s (entity) bank account.
  • Funds are released at the completion of a particular draw point upon completed inspection of work.
  • Generally, the period of time between initial draw request and money being wired is 1-3 days.

Payoff

  • No prepayment penalty.
  • Payoff is issued within 48 hours.
