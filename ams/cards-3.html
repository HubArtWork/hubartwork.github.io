Maybe you are planning your first move, or have not moved in many years, a long-distance moving can be an exciting, both or and unsettling experience. You've probably already realized that there's so much to do and so little time in which to do it.

There are personal considerations, family ties, old friendships, and for many of us, what to do about the children's schooling. At the same time, your move is most likely associated with an opportunity: promotion perhaps a new company or the chance to assume greater responsibility in job with a new locale.If you happen to be anovice mover, there are probably many questions that you need answered.
